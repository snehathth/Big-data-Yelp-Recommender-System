How This Works!

Once you click on your favorite restaurant, it triggers a ML model that calculates your next 3 recommendations based on the featrures of the restaurant you choose. It is a simple TFDIF Matrix calculation. You can scroll down to know more.

You can either click on REFRESH DATA if you are not happy with the choices or click MAKE RECOMMENDATION to see your next 3 choices.
