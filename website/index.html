How This Works!

Once you click on your favorite restaurant, it triggers a ML model that calculates your next 3 recommendations based on the features of the restaurant you choose. It is a simple TFD-IF Matrix calculation. You can scroll down to know more.

You can either click on REFRESH DATA if you are not happy with the choices or click MAKE RECOMMENDATION to see your next 3 choices.

The models behind

We have implemented a simple TF-IDF matrix. It is usually used in text analysis. Here we used different features as words to analyze our restaurants. When you enter your favorite restuarant, the matrix spins up a ranking system with restaurants that are close to your choice in terms of the price range, cuisine, if they serve alcohol, the dress code, if WiFi is available and a few other factors. Later, we have a function that sorts the matrix based on distance and gives the top 3 restuarants matching the chosen restuarant
